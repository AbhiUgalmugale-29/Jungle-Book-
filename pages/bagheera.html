Bagheera

Friends: Mowgli, Baloo, and the wolf pack.

Enemies: Shere Khan and the Bandar-log.

Favorite Food: Meat, as he is a panther.

Details: Bagheera is a black panther who serves as a protector and mentor to Mowgli. He is sleek, cunning, and wise, often providing a contrast to Baloo's more relaxed approach.
