Baloo

Friends: Mowgli, Bagheera, and other jungle animals.

Enemies: Shere Khan, although Baloo generally avoids making enemies.

Favorite Food: Honey, fruits, and roots.

Details: Baloo is a laid-back, kind-hearted bear who teaches Mowgli the Law of the Jungle. He values relaxation and enjoys a carefree lifestyle.
