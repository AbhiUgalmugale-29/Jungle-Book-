Mowgli

Friends: Baloo, Bagheera, Kaa, and the wolf pack.

Enemies: Shere Khan and, occasionally, the Bandar-log (monkey people).

Favorite Food: Fruits, berries, and jungle fare. Fruits and berries.

Details: Mowgli is a human child raised by wolves in the Indian jungle. He learns the ways of the jungle from his animal friends.
