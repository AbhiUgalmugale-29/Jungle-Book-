Meet the characters

Mowgli

I am Mowgli, a young boy who was raised by a wolves in a jungle

Baloo

Baloo, a sloth bear, is the strict teacher of the cubs of the Seeonee wolf pack.

Bagheera

He is a black panther (melanistic Indian leopard) who serves as friend, protector and mentor to the "man-cub" Mowgli.

Sherkhan

He is a powerful tiger who does not adhere to the Law of the Jungle like the other animals.

Tabaqui

Tabaqui is a Jackal with orange and white fur with golden eyes that lives in the jungle and an ally of Sher Khan.
